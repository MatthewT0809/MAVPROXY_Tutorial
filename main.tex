\section{Pre word}
It is to my astonishment when I first found out during my Group Design Project (GDP) that our most advanced flight arena in Cranfield does not have an actual written guide on using one of the most, if not, THE most valuable system within the flight arena, the VICON positioning system. For the duration of my GDP I've been asking around and seems like each PhD Student have their own way of connecting to VICON, which had led to a very incoherent and beginner unfriendly approach in using VICON when starting out. Hence, the creation of this guide! This glide's content is sourced from words of mouth from previous GDP member and PhD students, as well as some heuristic struggle from yours truly. By the end of this guide, the goal is that future cohorts from the MSc programme can figure out how to setup the Vicon Positioning System, setting up the Ground Control Station, setting up the drone, and implement custom pathplanning without doing much of the painful debugging progress or asking around. 

\vspace{0.5cm}

I would like to give a special thanks to Grace Elisha, Pol Padillla Moure, Luis Paula, and Ugurcan Celik for teaching me how this system works.  

\vspace{0.5cm}

For any non-dummies that understand this system much better than yours truly, please contact me from the email above for technicality refinement/comments/abuse.
